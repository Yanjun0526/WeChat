## Format Checkpoints

- Abstract: about 400-500 words, no visible page number, and focused on the report's work, major results, conclusions, and necessary method/scope details.
- Front matter: title page, abstract, declaration, optional acknowledgements, table of contents, list of figures, and list of tables.
- Title page: include university/department, programme, dissertation/final report label, thesis title, student name/number if required, supervisor, and submission date.
- Declaration: include own-work statement plus signature/name/date fields.
- Page numbering: roman numerals for front matter after the abstract; Arabic numbering restarts at Chapter 1.
- Layout: 25 mm margins where the template permits; 12 pt body text; clear paragraph indentation; double or spacious line spacing unless a later template overrides it.
- Captions: figure captions below figures; table titles above tables; avoid final full stops in caption titles unless needed.
- Numbering: figures, tables, and equations numbered by chapter.
- Ending sections: bibliography before appendices; conclusion includes findings, contributions/practical implications, limitations, and future work.
